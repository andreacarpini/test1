Moving FAQ

Get answers to the most frequently asked questions about moving services, pricing, and preparation.

General Questions

How long has Fairplay Moving been in business?

Fairplay Moving was founded in 2008 and has been serving the Chicago area for over 15 years. We've completed over 2,500 moves with a 98% customer satisfaction rate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We are fully licensed by the Illinois Commerce Commission (License: {{ site.license }}) and carry comprehensive insurance including general liability, cargo insurance, and workers' compensation.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Chicago metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs, including Schaumburg, Naperville, Evanston, Oak Brook, Arlington Heights, and more. We also handle long-distance moves to neighboring states.

Do you offer same-day moving services?

Yes, we offer same-day moving services when availability permits. However, we recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred date and time.

Pricing & Payment

How do you determine moving costs?

Our pricing is based on several factors: distance of the move, size/weight of items, number of movers needed, time required, and any additional services like packing or storage. We provide transparent, upfront quotes with no hidden fees.

Do you charge by the hour or flat rate?

Local moves (under 50 miles) are typically charged by the hour. Long-distance moves are usually flat-rate based on distance and weight. We'll explain the pricing structure clearly in your quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and certified checks. Payment is due upon completion of the move, unless other arrangements have been made.

Do you require a deposit?

For most local moves, we don't require a deposit. For larger moves or long-distance relocations, we may require a small deposit to secure your booking. This will be clearly stated in your quote.

Are there any hidden fees?

No! We believe in transparent pricing. Your quote will include all costs, and we'll explain any potential additional charges (like stairs, long carries, or specialty items) upfront.

Services

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes! We provide high-quality packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and tape. We can also pack your belongings for you with our professional packing service.

Can you move special items like pianos or artwork?

Absolutely! We have experience moving pianos, artwork, antiques, and other specialty items. We'll assess these items during your quote and use appropriate equipment and techniques.

Do you offer storage services?

Yes, we offer both short-term and long-term storage solutions in our secure, climate-controlled facility. Perfect for when your new home isn't quite ready.

Can you help with furniture assembly/disassembly?

Yes, our team can disassemble furniture for transport and reassemble it at your new location. This service is included in most moves at no extra charge.

Do you move appliances?

Yes, we can move most household appliances. For safety reasons, we ask that you disconnect and prepare appliances before the move. We can also help arrange for professional appliance servicing if needed.

Preparation & Moving Day

How should I prepare for moving day?

We recommend: labeling boxes clearly, disconnecting appliances, clearing pathways, and having a "first night" box with essentials. Our team will handle all the heavy lifting and loading.

Do I need to be present during the move?

Yes, we require someone to be present at both locations to answer questions, provide access, and sign off on the move completion.

What if it rains or snows on moving day?

We move in all weather conditions! Our team comes prepared with protective materials to keep your belongings dry and your home clean.

How long will my move take?

Move time depends on size, distance, and complexity. A typical 2-bedroom apartment move takes 4-6 hours. We'll provide a time estimate with your quote.

What should I do with valuables and personal items?

We recommend keeping important documents, jewelry, medications, and other valuables with you during the move. Our insurance covers belongings, but some items are irreplaceable.

Insurance & Claims

What insurance coverage do you provide?

We provide basic liability coverage at no charge, which covers items at $0.60 per pound. We also offer full replacement value coverage for additional protection. We'll explain all options during your quote.

What happens if something is damaged?

While we take every precaution, accidents can happen. Any damage claims should be reported within 15 days of your move. We'll work with you and our insurance to resolve issues quickly.

Can I purchase additional insurance?

Yes, we offer additional coverage options including full replacement value protection. We'll discuss these options and help you choose the right coverage for your needs.

Policies

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your move up to 48 hours before the scheduled date without penalty. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a small fee to cover preparation costs.

Do you have any items you won't move?

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot move hazardous materials, explosives, flammable items, or perishable foods. We'll provide a complete list during your quote consultation.

What are your operating hours?

We're available 7 days a week: Monday-Friday 8AM-6PM, Saturday 8AM-4PM, and Sunday by appointment. We can also accommodate after-hours moves for commercial clients.
