Before Moving Guide

Everything you need to know to prepare for a smooth, stress-free move. Follow our comprehensive guide to ensure nothing is overlooked.

Moving Timeline

Follow this timeline to stay organized and ensure your move goes smoothly from start to finish.

8W

8 Weeks Before Moving

  • Book your moving company and confirm dates
  • Create a moving budget and track expenses
  • Start decluttering and planning garage sale
  • Notify landlord if renting (30-60 day notice)
  • Research new schools, doctors, and services
6W

6 Weeks Before Moving

  • Order moving supplies (boxes, tape, bubble wrap)
  • Start using up frozen food and pantry items
  • Begin packing items you rarely use
  • Arrange for school transfers and records
  • Schedule time off work for moving day
4W

4 Weeks Before Moving

  • Confirm moving details with Fairplay Moving
  • Update address with post office (USPS.com)
  • Notify banks, credit cards, and subscriptions
  • Arrange utility transfers (electric, gas, water)
  • Continue packing non-essential items
  • Plan meals to use up remaining food
  • Measure furniture and doorways
  • Plan new furniture layout
2W

2 Weeks Before Moving

  • Confirm parking arrangements for moving truck
  • Begin packing most items except daily essentials
  • Label all boxes with contents and destination room
  • Arrange for pet and child care on moving day
  • Return borrowed items and library books
1W

Moving Week

  • Finish packing all non-essential items
  • Confirm details with Fairplay Moving
  • Pack "first night" essentials box
  • Defrost refrigerator and freezer
  • Prepare appliances for moving
  • Clean current home thoroughly
  • Charge phone and prepare snacks
  • Get cash for tips and unexpected expenses
  • Get good night's sleep before moving day
  • Prepare keys and access information

Expert Packing Tips

Professional packing techniques to protect your belongings and make unpacking easier.

Quality Materials

  • • Use sturdy boxes in various sizes
  • • Heavy items in small boxes, light items in large boxes
  • • Use packing paper, not newspaper (ink transfers)
  • • Quality packing tape, not masking tape
  • • Bubble wrap for fragile items

Smart Labeling

  • • Label boxes on all sides and top
  • • Use color coding for different rooms
  • • Mark fragile items clearly
  • • Number boxes and keep inventory list
  • • Mark "load last" for essentials

Fragile Items

  • • Wrap each item individually
  • • Use plenty of cushioning material
  • • Pack plates vertically, not stacked
  • • Fill all empty spaces in boxes
  • • Double-box extremely fragile items

Moving Day Success

Make moving day go smoothly with these essential tips and preparations.

Essential Box Contents

First Night Essentials

  • • Toiletries and medications
  • • Change of clothes and pajamas
  • • Phone chargers and electronics
  • • Important documents
  • • Basic cleaning supplies

Kitchen Essentials

  • • Paper plates and disposable utensils
  • • Coffee maker and supplies
  • • Snacks and bottled water
  • • Basic cookware
  • • Dish soap and sponges

Tools & Hardware

  • • Basic tool set
  • • Light bulbs and batteries
  • • Tape measure
  • • Scissors or box cutter
  • • Garbage bags

Moving Day Checklist

1

Be Ready Early

Wake up early, have coffee ready, and be prepared for the movers' arrival.

2

Clear Pathways

Ensure clear paths from rooms to the moving truck. Remove obstacles and rugs.

3

Protect Your Home

Lay down protective coverings on floors and protect door frames.

4

Stay Accessible

Be available to answer questions and direct movers to the right rooms.

5

Final Walkthrough

Do a final check of every room, closet, and cabinet before leaving.
