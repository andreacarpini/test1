Our Story

Fairplay Moving was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: to provide Chicago residents and businesses with honest, reliable, and professional moving services. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into one of Chicago's most trusted moving companies.

Our founder, Michael Rodriguez, believed that moving shouldn't be a stressful experience. With over 15 years in the moving industry, he understood the challenges people face during relocations and set out to create a company that truly puts customers first.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers each year, maintaining our commitment to fair pricing, professional service, and treating every customer's belongings as if they were our own.
